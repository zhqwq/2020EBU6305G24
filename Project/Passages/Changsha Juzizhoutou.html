Juzizhoutou, Changsha

Juzizhou Island, located in the centre of the Xiangjiang River in the Yuelu District of Changsha City, Hunan Province, originally covering an area of about 17 hectares, is the largest of the many alluvial sandbars in the lower reaches of the Xiangjiang River, and is known as "China's First Island". Surrounded by water on all sides, it stretches for more than ten miles and is about 40 metres across at the narrowest point and 140 metres across at the widest point, in the shape of a long island.

The Juzizhou Island is home to the Mao Zedong Youth Art Sculpture and the Ask the Sky Terrace. History tells us that the Juzizhou Island was created in the second year of Emperor Huixing's reign (305) as a result of the swirling alluvial currents and the accumulation of sand and gravel. Thousands of species of flowering plants and vines grow in the landscape, including 143 species of valuable plants. There are also gulls, foxes, badgers and many other rare animals.

Juzizhou Island covers an area of 0.9164 square kilometres, including Juzizhou Island and Fujiazhou, with a total built-up area of about 1.3834 square kilometres.

Juzizhou Island has a subtropical monsoon climate with hot and rainy summers and mild and less rainy winters, making the climate humid and pleasant. The average annual temperature ranges between 13°C and 20°C. The average temperature in January is above 0°C. The construction structure of Juzizhou Island is planned as "one axis and four zones", with one axis being the tourism axis running from north to south, and the four zones being the core scenic area, the tourism support service area, the beach park and the water sports area. The main attractions are built around the theme of "ecology, culture, tourism and leisure", including the Juzizhou Pavilion at the head of the island, the Chinese white jade poetry monument, the Bronze Statue Square, the Vine Square, the memorial site for Chairman Mao's trip to the Xiangjiang River, the Ruyue Pavilion, the Pillow River Pavilion, the Bonsai Garden and the Gate Square.
