Wukesong, Beijing

Cadillac Center
Cadillac center is located in the northeast corner of Wukesong bridge, West Fourth Ring Road, Beijing, with a construction area of 63000 square meters. It is the basketball competition venue for the 2008 Beijing Olympic Games and the first Olympic venue in China to be named and sponsored. Today, Cadillac center has become a gathering place for cultural and entertainment activities in Beijing. It holds hundreds of concerts, sports events and other large-scale activities every year, and the utilization rate of venues is as high as 70%. It is a model for the utilization of Olympic venues after the games.

The external curtain wall is the first passive curtain wall in China to adopt the traditional aluminum alloy curtain wall system and optimize the process design to make it a passive curtain wall with the comprehensive heat transfer coefficient K less than 1.0.

Commercial Street
Huaxi live commercial district is the supporting facilities of Wukesong stadium group, adjacent to the West Fourth Ring Road in the west, Fuxing Road in the south, and connected with Wukesong station of Metro Line 1, with a total land area of 123100 square meters. The center is a large-scale activity square, surrounded by more than 100 fashion brands, including restaurants, bars, cultural experience stores, live house and sports brand flagship stores with different tastes. It is the second "characteristic Commercial Street" in Beijing.

M-space
M space, located in the south of Cadillac center, is the basketball training hall of 2008 Olympic Games, which has been transformed into a multi-function hall after Beijing Olympic Games. The unique column free and sinking design makes it an ideal venue for all kinds of activities. M space held more than 80 activities throughout the year, with the utilization rate of more than 65%.

Hi-park basketball park
Wukesong hi-park basketball park is located in the southwest of Cadillac center, covering an area of 15200 square meters. It has 11 basketball courts and an air cage half court. As an upgrade of the mass basketball park, since its completion in 2014, hi-park has rapidly become a gathering place for basketball stars, popular events, high-end training camps and trendy cultural activities.

Beijing Times Art Museum
Beijing Times Art Museum is a private public welfare Art Museum wholly-owned by Huaxi Group, with a construction area of about 6000 square meters and a mobile exhibition line of about 672 meters. The main structure of the exhibition hall is composed of a number of irregular spaces, showing not only the modern and future free form, but also the cohesive and open visual tension and rich imagination.

Huaxi live center
Huaxi live center is a comprehensive building for Wukesong Sports and cultural industry. It is located in the northeast corner of Cadillac center, Haidian District, Beijing. It is adjacent to Yuyuantan South Road in the north, Xicui road in the East, and an internal road separated from Cadillac center in the West. Huaxi live center is L-shaped, with a total construction area of 70000 square meters. There are three office buildings on the ground, namely block a, block B and block C. The three buildings are connected by corridors on the third and ninth floors; There is a sunken courtyard on the first floor of the underground along the periphery of the building to connect the three buildings. The second and third floors of the underground are garages, with a total of about 350 parking spaces.
