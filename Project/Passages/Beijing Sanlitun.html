Sanlitun, Beijing

Sanlitun is located in the east of Chaoyang District. It is three li away from the inner city. Now it is famous for Sanlitun Bar Street. Sanlitun Bar Street is one of the more "prosperous" entertainment streets in Beijing's nightlife. It is a place frequented by foreigners and domestic celebrities living in Beijing. The Ming and Qing Dynasties belonged to Daxing County of shuntianfu. As a film title, it generally refers to the area surrounded by Zuojiazhuang, Chaowai Street, Hujialou and Maizidian. In 1962, the North embassy area was built in the region. From 1960s to 1970s, the diplomatic apartment group was built. Sanlitun area gradually developed into an important community for diplomats in China to live, shop and foreign affairs activities.

There are 10 vertical and 12 horizontal streets in the area. Xindong road and worker's Stadium East Road are part of the capital's former Yingbin Road. Sanlitun Bar Street is one of the main places of nightlife in Beijing. There are Tayuan diplomatic staff apartment, Yansha friendship mall, foreign exchange duty-free shop, China International store, 3.3 clothing building and other units in the area. There are City Hotel, Zhaolong Hotel and Qilin building on both sides of the east section of worker's Stadium North Road. Sanlitun has Sanlitun Dongli in the East and Sanlitun Nanli in the south. The northwest of the village is the happy three villages.
